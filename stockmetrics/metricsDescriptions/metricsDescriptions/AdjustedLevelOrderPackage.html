Calculates the adjusted level order of a packages. The adjusted level order is defined as 0 for packages which are dependent on no other project packages. Packages with dependencies have level order equal to the maximum level order of their dependency packages, plus the number of packages on which they have mutual or cyclic dependencies. Conceptually, the adjusted level order measure how many "layers" of software a package depends on, highlighting especially those with cyclic dependencies. Packages with large adjusted level orders may be difficult to test and maintain.
Abbreviation: Level*
